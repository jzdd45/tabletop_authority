Xia: Legends of a Drift System

Xia is a crowdfunding sucess story meeting all of its stretch goals. The game features a sci-fi themed game filled with strategy, adventure, and a bit of luck. You are a rookie pilot looking to make your mark on the galaxy, how you achieve that is up to you. You can play as a low-down criminal who will do anything for a payday or a law abiding citizen looking to make an honest living hunting down scum, trading, or helping out the common man. You can even do either or a combination of both, the choice is yours!

My review:

This is one of my favorite games as it is not only wonderfully detailed and high quality but it is also a blast to play with friends and family. It has a bit of a learning curve but once mastered this game is quite easy to play. The game has a lot of replayability since there are many sector tiles that are randomly chosen to expand the map as well as, the diverse selection of ships, mission cards, and abilities. Rating: 9.5/10
