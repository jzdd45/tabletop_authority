Pandemic

Pandemic is a cooperative game that has players assume different roles of disease fighting scientists to stop a massive outbreak from destroying the world. The game is very easy to learn and is a great team game for the whole family. The production value of the game is very high and it has a lot of replayability.

My review:

This game is one of the most simple and well thought out approaches to cooperative gaming I have ever played. It can be played with friends and family over and over again without ever getting old. The paid expansions also provide several fun new mechanics to keep the game interesting. Rating: 8/10
