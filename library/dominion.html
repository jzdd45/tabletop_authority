Dominion

Dominion is a competitive and fast-paced card game set in a fantasy world. Your goal is to be the player with the biggest kingdom at the end of the game. You customize your deck by buying cards from a pool in the playing area. Then you play the cards you have in your hand to make the most of every turn in order to become the greatest monarch in all the land.

My review:
