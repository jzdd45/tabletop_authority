Axis & Allies: 1942

Axis & Allies: 1942 is a srategy game set in World War II. The game includes a set of unique and detailed units for each country to command. You can lead either the Axis or the Allies and battle your friends and family to see who will win one of the most pivotal wars in history.

My review:

This game is essentially a more complicated version of Risk. Although it may take several hours, it is a very accurate and fun strategic game for serious gamers. Rating: 7/10
